Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Built for engineers who document as they build. No accounts, no cloud sync, no distractions.

Daily Entries

Entries organized by day with timestamps. Hit  ⌘J  to start a new entry instantly.

Rich Text Editing

Powered by BlockNote. Type  /  for slash commands — headings, lists, code blocks, and more.

Inline Drawings

Embed Excalidraw diagrams right in your notes with the  /drawing  command. Resizable canvases.

Tagging System

Categorize entries with color-coded tags. Click any tag in the sidebar to filter your notes instantly.

Full-Text Search

Search across all entries, content, and tags. Press  ⌘K  for instant results.

Local-First & Private

All data stored in your browser's IndexedDB. No server, no accounts, no tracking. Your notes stay yours.

Import & Export

Export all your entries as JSON for backup or migration. Import them back anytime — your data is always portable.

Archive & Restore

Archive old entries to keep your workspace clean. Browse, search, and restore them anytime from the archive view.

Wiki-Style Linking

Type [[ to link between entries. Click any link to jump straight to the referenced note.

How it works

Open. Write. Done.

No sign-up, no install. Just open the app and start documenting your engineering work.

1

Open the app

Go to workledger.org — no sign-up, no install. Or self-host from the repo.

2

Write & draw

Create entries with rich text, embed diagrams, and tag your notes. Everything auto-saves locally.

3

Search & filter

Find anything instantly with full-text search, tag filtering, and keyboard shortcuts.

Optional · v1.1

Think with AI

Click the lightbulb next to any note, choose from 10 structured thinking frameworks, and let an open-source LLM challenge your assumptions, find blind spots, and stress-test your ideas. Runs locally via Ollama or remotely via Hugging Face — your choice, your data.

1

Enable AI

Click the gear icon in the sidebar and select "Enable AI"

2

Pick a provider

Connect Ollama locally, Hugging Face remotely, or your own server

3

Click the lightbulb

Hover over any note and click the lightbulb icon to open the AI sidebar

4

Choose a framework

Pick one of 10 thinking models and start a structured conversation

Choose a thinking framework

Click the lightbulb on any note, then pick a framework — from First Principles to Six Thinking Hats.

Structured AI conversation

The framework is applied to your note's content. Switch steps, ask follow-ups, copy results.
